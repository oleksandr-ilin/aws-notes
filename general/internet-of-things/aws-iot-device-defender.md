Overview
Security auditing and monitoring service for IoT fleets that detects compromised devices and policy violations.

Tasks
- Enable fleet audits, configure detectors and alerts, and integrate with incident workflows.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Custom telemetry analysis | Flexible | High ops cost |
| Third‑party IoT security tools | Specialized features | Integration effort & cost |

Limitations
- Focused on IoT-specific telemetry; requires instrumentation and tuning to reduce false positives.

Price info
- Charged per device assessment and data processed.

Network & multi-region
- Enable per-region with centralized findings aggregation for multi-account fleets.

Popular use cases
- Fleet compromise detection, compliance checks for device configurations.

When Not to Use
- Tiny fleets where manual checks suffice.

DR strategy
- Archive findings and ensure playbooks and remediation scripts are versioned and testable.
