Overview
Neural machine translation service for many languages with batch and real-time APIs.

Tasks
- Submit text for translation, manage custom terminology and glossary, and integrate results.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Open-source MT + custom models | Control & privacy | Requires resources to maintain |
| Google Translate API | Different language models | Vendor choice |

Limitations
- Domain-specific terminology may need custom glossaries; nuance can be lost.

Price info
- Per-character pricing for translation requests.

Network & multi-region
- Regional endpoints; route translation requests to lowest-latency region.

Popular use cases
- Localization, content translation, and multilingual chat.

When Not to Use
- Highly specialized legal/medical translations requiring certified translators.

DR strategy
- Archive source & translated texts and glossary configs for reprocessing.
