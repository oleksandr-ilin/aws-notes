Overview
Tooling to manage and track software licenses across AWS and on-prem resources.

Tasks
- Register licenses, apply usage rules, and generate compliance reports.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Manual license tracking | Simplicity | Error-prone

Limitations
- Vendor-specific license models may need custom handling.

Price info
- Typically free; integration costs may apply.

Network & multi-region
- Regional; centralize reporting where needed.

Popular use cases
- BYOL management and license compliance reporting.

When Not to Use
- Small environments where manual tracking suffices.

DR strategy
- Backup license inventories and reports to versioned storage.
