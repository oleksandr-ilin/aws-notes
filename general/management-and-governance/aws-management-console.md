Overview
Web-based management console for browsing and managing AWS resources.

Tasks
- Use console for human-friendly operations, troubleshooting and resource inspection.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| AWS CLI / SDKs | Scriptable & automatable | Not as interactive

Limitations
- Not suitable for automation or large-scale changes; prone to human error.

Price info
- Free; incurred costs are from actions taken through the console.

Network & multi-region
- Global access to account; some services show region-specific views.

Popular use cases
- Ad-hoc ops, dashboards, and quick configuration checks.

When Not to Use
- For repeatable infra changes—use IaC or automation.

DR strategy
- Keep critical runbooks and IAM recovery procedures documented and tested.
