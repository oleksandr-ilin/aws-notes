Overview
Personalized dashboard with health events and guidance for the AWS resources in your account.

Tasks
- Monitor active events, subscribe to notifications, and map health events to runbooks.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Public status pages | General info | Not account-specific

Limitations
- Account-specific; does not replace broader incident management tools.

Price info
- Free; advanced guidance may come with support plans.

Network & multi-region
- Global; provides account-specific health info across regions.

Popular use cases
- Incident awareness and mapping provider issues to account impact.

When Not to Use
- Public operational monitoring for multiple providers.

DR strategy
- Archive events and link to incident postmortems and runbooks in version control.
