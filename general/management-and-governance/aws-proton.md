Overview
Managed delivery service for platform teams to define and provision standardized infrastructure patterns.

Tasks
- Define environment and service templates, manage deployments and lifecycle of platform products.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Custom platform tooling | Fully tailored | Build & maintenance cost

Limitations
- Template authoring and lifecycle management have a learning curve.

Price info
- No separate charge; underlying resources billed.

Network & multi-region
- Regional; design templates for multi-region deployments where needed.

Popular use cases
- Internal developer platforms, standardizing infrastructure patterns across teams.

When Not to Use
- Small teams without platform engineering needs.

DR strategy
- Backup templates as code and version product releases for rollback.
