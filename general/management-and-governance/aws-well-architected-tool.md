Overview
Run Well-Architected reviews and generate improvement plans aligned to AWS best practices.

Tasks
- Run workload reviews, capture answers, and prioritize improvements by pillar.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Third-party review frameworks | Different perspectives | Less AWS-specific guidance

Limitations
- Advisory only; it does not enforce fixes.

Price info
- Free to use; professional engagement costs for remediation may apply.

Network & multi-region
- Regional; assessments are per-workload and can cover multi-region designs.

Popular use cases
- Architecture reviews, remediation plans, and risk identification.

When Not to Use
- Quick tactical changes without strategic architecture review needs.

DR strategy
- Save review outputs and remediation plans in version control and track progress.
