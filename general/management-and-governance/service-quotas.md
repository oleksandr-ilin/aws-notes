Overview
Service to view and request increases for AWS service limits and quotas.

Tasks
- Check current quotas, request increases, and monitor usage against limits.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Manual tracking | Simple | Risk of surprise limits

Limitations
- Some quotas require justification or have hard limits.

Price info
- Free to view and request increases; provisioning costs for resources still apply.

Network & multi-region
- Regional quotas for many services; plan increases in target regions.

Popular use cases
- Ensuring capacity for deployments and avoiding throttling due to default limits.

When Not to Use
- For ad-hoc small deployments not near quota thresholds.

DR strategy
- Document and store quota requests and approval evidence in project records.
