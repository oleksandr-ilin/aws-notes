Overview
DDoS protection service: Shield Standard (free) and Shield Advanced (paid) for enhanced detection and response.

Tasks
- Subscribe Shield Advanced, integrate with WAF and response playbooks.

Alternatives
| Alternative | Main pro | Main con |
|---|---:|---|
| Third‑party DDoS providers | Specialized mitigation | Cost & integration |

Limitations
- Shield Advanced requires subscription and may need SOC workflows for response.

Price info
- Shield Standard included; Shield Advanced is a paid subscription plus data transfer costs.

Network & multi-region
- Protect regional endpoints and CloudFront distributions; coordinate with Global Accelerator if used.

Popular use cases
- Protect high-profile web properties and APIs from volumetric attacks.

When Not to Use
- Low-traffic internal services where costs outweigh benefits.

DR strategy
- Ensure DDoS playbooks, contact lists, and mitigations are documented and tested.
